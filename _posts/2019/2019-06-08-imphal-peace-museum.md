The [Imphal Peace Museum](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Imphal_Peace_Museum) ([photos](https://photos.app.goo.gl/Gao3hq1qYsgNBnzy6)) is a World War II museum at the foothills of the Red Hills (Maibam Lokpa Ching) in [Manipur](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Manipur), India. It is a living memory of the Battle of Imphal (Anglo-Japanese war) and other World War II battles (Marchâ€“July 1944) fought in Manipur.
